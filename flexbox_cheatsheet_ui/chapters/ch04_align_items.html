Align Items

align-items distributes space along the cross axis (perpendicular to flex-direction). It controls how items are aligned within each row/column.

All Values

stretch (default)
Items stretch to fill container height along cross axis
flex-start
Items aligned to the start of the cross axis
flex-end
Items aligned to the end of the cross axis
center
Items centered along the cross axis
baseline
Text baselines of items are aligned
